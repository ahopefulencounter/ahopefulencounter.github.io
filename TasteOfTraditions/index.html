About Our Program

A Hopeful Encounter presents an immersive culinary experience celebrating Hmong traditions.

Our 11-month cooking program goes beyond recipes to strengthen family connections, preserve cultural heritage, and promote healthy eating through traditional Hmong cuisine.

Location

Duration

11-month program with weekly sessions

What You'll Learn

Cultural cooking skills that nourish both body and family connections

Family Bonding

Strengthen intergenerational connections through shared cooking experiences

Parent/Child Relationships

Healthy Eating

Essential Skills

Master fundamental Hmong cooking techniques passed through generations

Historical Context

Understand the stories and significance behind traditional dishes

Creativity & Confidence

Develop culinary creativity while building self-assurance in the kitchen
