About Our Trunk or Treat

Join A Hopeful Encounter for our annual Trunk or Treat event at Midtown Plaza! This family-friendly Halloween celebration provides a safe environment for children to enjoy trick-or-treating from creatively decorated vehicle trunks while connecting with our community.

This event supports our Taste of Traditions program, which builds holistic relationships between parents and children through culinary experiences that honor our cultural heritage.

For Families

Free admission for children 12 and under. Bring your own trick-or-treat bags! Costumes encouraged for all ages.

For Participants

Vendors, performers, and car show participants must register in advance. Limited spaces available!

Pho Fundraiser

Enjoy delicious traditional pho at our fundraiser booth! All proceeds support our Taste of Traditions program, which builds holistic relationships between parents and children through culinary experiences that honor our cultural heritage.

Taste of Traditions brings generations together in the kitchen, preserving family recipes while creating new memories and strengthening bonds through the shared experience of cooking and eating together.

Only $10 per bowl!
