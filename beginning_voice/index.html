Our Mission

Beginning Voice uses music as a tool for youth empowerment, mental wellness, and social change.

Inspired by the healing power of music, our program provides a safe space for young people to:

  • Develop confidence through artistic expression
  • Build emotional resilience and coping skills
  • Find community with peers facing similar challenges
  • Amplify their voices on issues that matter to them

The Beginning Voice Experience

Our 16-week program combines music education with mental health support

Vocal Training

Professional instruction in:

  • Breath control & technique
  • Vocal health & maintenance
  • Performance skills
  • Songwriting basics

Wellness Components

Integrated mental health support:

  • Emotional expression through music
  • Mindfulness & stress reduction
  • Peer support circles
  • Referrals to counseling services

Advocacy Training

Using music for social change:

  • Writing songs about community issues
  • Performing at local events
  • Youth leadership development
  • Connecting with activist networks
